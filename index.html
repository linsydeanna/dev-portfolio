About Me

Hi! I'm Linsy. I love learning about programming theory, writing clean code, and creating fast applications. I enjoy sharing knowledge through talks and collaborating with others to level up as a developer and help others do the same.

Learning is what gives my life and career meaning every day. In my early twenties I performed aerial acrobatics professionally, and in between shows I studied various topics, with a focus on physical and mental health. During graduate school I became fascinated by personality theory and led various workshops to educate others on how to use their psychological type to be successful in their career. Studying my own type opened my eyes to my natural programming ability. My background has allowed me a high level of empathy for the users I craft interfaces for as well as strong collaborative skills that allow me to work well on software development teams.
